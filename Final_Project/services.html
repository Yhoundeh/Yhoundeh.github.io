Our motel offers many free convenient services suited to your needs:

Amenaties

We provide free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes.

Playroom

A supervised playroom kitted out with church history inspired toys is perfect for your kids ages 3-8. Additionally, we have partnered with Bethesda Babysitting for low-cost, short-term babysitting services for the children of patrons.

Missionary Accommodations

We have long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites available for full-time temple missionaries. Missionary discounts and pricing arrangements are available.

Missionary Apartments

Sitting Area

For those interested in history or who want a location for a good chat we have a temple history themed reception and sitting area.

Reception Hall

A wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving. Additionally we offer full catering through our partnership with Amberose Catering.

Reception Hall

Family Search Center

And finally last but not least we offer a complete A family search center that supports all kinds of family search and ordinance work. On Tuesdays we have experienced volunteers who could help you find your next big breakthrough in your family line.
