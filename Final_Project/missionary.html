Missionary Appartments

We recently installed long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries. We strive to give a generous discount for Full Time Missionaries. This varies by the month as shown in the chart to the left with normal charges on the left side for comparison.

In addition our family search center that supports family search and ordinance work and has serves as a useful contact point with nonmembers. Missionaries may sign up for service activities in the family search center. However, due to state and local Covid-19 restrictions, additional safety measures may be required. As per church protocol a face mask is required to be present and worn while missionaries are interacting with the public. For more information call or write us.
