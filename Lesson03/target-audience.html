The Target Audience

The audience that would want to come visit this website would be those who live in the area that is covered by the website and would like to know about the Weather either it be what the current weather is like, what the weather would be like later on in the day, or what the weekly forcast is for the current week. The people living in the area and those planning on visiting the area are those who would be interesting in this website.

Personas

  1. This person is 35 year old male farmer who lives in the near one of the towns that is covered by this website. He is trying to plan on when to water his crops but he wants to know if it going to rain later on in the week. So he would visit this site were he would get the information that he needs through the weekly forcast and thus would be able to plan accordingly.
  2. This person is a 25 year old male outdoors man who in interested on going fishing at one of the lakes that are nearby the towns that are covered by this website. He is interested on knowing if it will rain on the Saturday he plans on going fishing. He will want to visit this website so he can get the information that he needs using the weekly forcast and thus would be able to plan accordingly.

Scenarios

  • I need to drive to town today to get some shopping done but I need to know what the weather is like right now.
  • I plan on going camping later this week but I don't know what the weather is going to be like.
  • I'm wondering if I should walk my dog in the morning or in the afternoon but I don't know when it is going to rain today.
  • I'm wondering if I should go to the football or just watch it on TV, I just really don't want to get stuck in the rain.
  • I'm wondering if I should have the kids walk to school or if I should drive them there. I don't want them walking in a thunderstorm.
