The Target Audience

The target audience for this website is those who are interested in or enjoy watching weather phennomena. Particularly people who are interested in their local weather such as those living in states subject to hurricanes or tornados.

Personas

  1. This person is a 17 year old female high school student who is working on a science fair project about local weather patterns. She wants to know what the most extreame frm of weather is in her local area to make her project more interesting.
  2. This person is a 20 year old male outdoors man who enjoys cloud watching. He wants to know when the most exciting thunderstorms of the year are heading in. He also wants to know what direction and time that storm could be expected.

Scenarios

  • My younger sibling is nervous about storms. I need information to show that weather is not that severe here, and that it can be fun.
  • I plan on going cloud watching as a date and I need a rough time-table.
  • I am writting a novel and i need to know what the worst snow storm in Idaho was in the last hundred years to base a plot point off of.
  • I saw a weather channel documentry today. They made some incredable claims. I want to know more about this becuse it sounds neat.
  • The farmers say that last year was the worst snowstorm of the century. I don't belive that, and I would like to know if they are correct.
