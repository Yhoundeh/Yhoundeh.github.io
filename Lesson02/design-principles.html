Visual Hierarchy

Youtube

https://www.youtube.com/

I choose youtube for visual hierarchy as each level produces videos to try and catch your attention. Ads are placed directly at the top of the page in mobile to catch visitor's attention right off the bat.

PARC: Proximity

Amazon

https://www.amazon.com/

Amazon's site does a good job of placing photos of like items together and in complemetry layouts. The furniture section has a good example with pictures of chairs and couches organized into a picture "window."

Whitespace and Clean Design

Science Daily

https://www.sciencedaily.com/

Science Daily is interesting becuse their previous site was very cluttered with a dark claustrophobic header. Now the site has been redone in shades of white and grey. This helps create an open atmoshphere that was lacking before.
