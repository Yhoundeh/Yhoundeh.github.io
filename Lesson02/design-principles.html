Visual Hierarchy

The Microsoft Store

https://www.microsoft.com/

I choose the Microsoft store for visual hierarchy because the site uses it when displaying the products that Microsoft is trying to sell. You would see one main product like the surface laptop and then below you would then see product like headpoints and software that would go work with the surface laptop at a smaller size.

White Space and Clean Design

The Apple Store

https://www.apple.com/shop

I choose the Apple store for white space and clean design because all of Apple's websites and products follow this very well. If you look at each part of the page, you'll see lots of whitespace between each part and the design layout is very clean and not cluddered.

PARC: Proximity

Raspberry Pi Home Page

https://www.raspberrypi.org/

I choose the Raspberry Pi home page because they have a lot of content on the screen and to help make it more navigatable, they group links and products together that are more similar or on the same topic and they seprate different items or products by putting them in different boxs or putting lines between them.
